FAQs

Q: What exactly is GradeEasy?
A: GradeEasy is a user-friendly web application designed to help university students easily calculate and track their Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA). It takes into account different grading components and allows for customization based on university-specific grading systems.
Q: What makes GradeEasy different from other CGPA calculators?
A: GradeEasy stands out due to its customizable settings that allow students to adapt the calculator to their university's unique grading rules. It also shows performance graphs, saves semester data locally, providing easy access without requiring an internet connection or sign-up.
Q: My university has a different grading system. Can I still use GradeEasy?
A: Absolutely! GradeEasy includes a customizable settings panel where you can create custom grading presets, define mark distribution (e.g., total marks, credit hours), and set grading scales (e.g., percentage ranges, GPA, and letter grades) to match your university‚Äôs system. In the Settings tab, click the + Add new Preset button to open the preset modal. Enter a preset name, specify total marks and credit hours, define the grading table (start/end percentages, GPA, and grade), and click "Apply" to save the preset.
Q: What is the formula GradeEasy uses to calculate CGPA?
A: GradeEasy calculates CGPA using the formula: $$ \text{CGPA} = \frac{\sum (\text{Course Grade} \times \text{Credit Hour})}{\sum (\text{Credit Hour})} $$
Q: Can you explain the calculation process step-by-step?
A: Here's how GradeEasy calculates your CGPA:
  • It converts the total marks for each course into corresponding grade points based on the defined grading scale.
  • It multiplies the grade points for each course by its respective credit hour.
  • It sums up all the (grade points √ó credit hours) for all courses.
  • Finally, it divides this sum by the total sum of all credit hours to get the final CGPA.
Q: What kind of customizations can I make in the Settings Tab?
A: In the Settings Tab, you can create custom grading presets, define mark distribution for different assessment components, and adjust other settings.
Q: How does the Track CGPA tab work?
A: The Track CGPA Tab stores your semester-wise CGPA records and visualizes your progress over time using line graphs. You can also view detailed reports for each semester. Remember to check Track this semester in the CG Calculate tab to track it here.

Note: This CGPA is for S-CGPA (Semester CGPA), not the final CGPA of your entire Honors life! üòé To calculate your overall Honors CGPA, use the formula: \[ \frac{\sum (\text{Semester CGPA} \times \text{Semester Credit Hours})}{\text{Total Credit Hours}} \] Put some pressure on yourself, broo, and track those grades like a pro! üöÄ

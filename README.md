## 🚀 Introduction

**GradeEasy** is a simple and open-source CGPA calculator made especially for university students.  
Just input your course marks, and GradeEasy will handle the CGPA calculation and tracking — no spreadsheets or complex formulas required.


## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **CGPA Calculator** — Enter your marks (CA, Mid, Final) and credit hours.
- 📊 **Track CGPA** — Save semester results and visualize your progress over time.
- ⚙️ **Custom Grading Presets** — Includes BDU's default, with flexibility to modify.
- 📘 **Department Info** — Built-in course details for Software Engineering at BDU.
- 💾 **Local Storage** — Offline-first experience. Your data stays in your browser.
